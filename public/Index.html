Track your Emplyees efficiantly

team view in map

Welcome to Origgo, the services we offer are for big and small companies alike. We provide a channel between employers and their employees for knowing whereabouts under traveling times.

How it works:
We get all our flight information from Aviation Edge API and presents it to the user in an easy and readable fashion, we give the services to build a team and assign flight to the individuals, this allows for employers to see where Their employees are. This gives an edge on planning for traveling and easy to use interface for the Employees. Never miss a flight or lose an employee again! When our partnership is established, you will get a certificated key that verifies you as an authorized user. Use that key when you sign up in the page and provide some basic information of yourself and your company name. That’s it! We are set to build our own team!
